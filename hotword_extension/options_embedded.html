Help

For speech recognition to work, you need to allow microphone access in Chrome settings.

Make sure your microphone is connected and turned on.

This is an add-on for Voice Actions for Chrome. You can uninstall “OK Chrome” for Voice Actions for Chrome to disable the “OK Chrome” hotword and still use the rest of Voice Actions for Chrome.

Activation

“OK Chrome” activation is a premium feature for Voice Actions for Chrome for supporters. Sign in with Ko-fi or Patreon to activate it.
