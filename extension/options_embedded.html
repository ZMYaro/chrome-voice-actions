Help

For speech recognition to work, you need to allow microphone access in Chrome settings.

Make sure your microphone is connected and turned on.

Try these different types of actions:

  • “Pictures of sunsets”

  • “Videos of cats”

  • “Map of
    Washington, D.C.”

  • “Directions to
    The White House”

  • “Go to Wikipedia.”

  • “Listen to Deadmau5”

  • “Calculate 11 × 8”

  • “Switch to [tab]”

  • “Open settings”

  • Or just search for anything

Hotword

Install and enable the “OK Chrome” for Voice Actions for Chrome extension to have Voice Actions for Chrome activate when you say, “OK Chrome”. Enabling this feature may use more resources.

Voice Actions for Chrome will activate when you say, “OK Chrome”.
This feature may use more resources. You can disable or uninstall the “OK Chrome” for Voice Actions for Chrome extension at any time to disable this feature. The main Voice Actions for Chrome extension will never listen in the background.
