Voice Actions for Chrome has updated!

Voice Actions for Chrome 5 keeps the same familiar experience with some clean-up, polish, and exciting new features, including:

  • Hotword

    Install the separate “OK Chrome” premium add-on to activate Voice Actions with just your voice!

  • New actions

    More sites are now available to open with Voice Actions, along with new commands to open Chrome settings or history.

  • New options

    Have Voice Actions perform actions instantly, and change the microphone button to a dark, light, or minimalist variant—whatever looks best with your Chrome theme!
